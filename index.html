I'm a Computer Science Engineering student at Indore Institute of Science and Technology (IIST), graduating in May 2027 with a CGPA of 8.23 and no active backlogs. My work sits at the intersection of generative AI, data science, and full-stack engineering.

I've shipped two live AI products — Verbalystic, a real-time AI public speaking coach, and reglens-ai, an LLM-powered regulatory compliance assistant for FinTechs. I presented my research on Verbalystic at the IGNITEX-2025 National Conference and reached the Semi-Finals of ET AI Hackathon 2026 by The Economic Times.
